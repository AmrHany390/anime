Monkey D. Luffy

King of the pirates

Biography

Monkey D. Luffy, the spirited captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, set out from the East Blue with a single goal: to find the legendary One Piece and become the Pirate King. After eating the Gomu Gomu no Mi, he gained the power to stretch like rubber, which he uses to protect his crew and liberate oppressed islands. Known for his infectious smile and unwavering loyalty, Luffy’s true strength lies in his ability to turn everyone he meets into an ally, making him the most free man on the sea.
