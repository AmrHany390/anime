EREN YEAGER

The embodiment of freedom

Biography

Eren Yeager is the inheritor of the Attack Titan and the Founding Titan. Driven by a fierce desire for freedom, Eren fights to protect his friends and uncover the truth about the titans. As the series progresses, he evolves from a passionate but impulsive young man into a complex and determined figure who holds the power to change the world. Eren's unyielding resolve and overwhelming power make him one of anime's most iconic characters.
