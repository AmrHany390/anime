Naruto Uzumaki

The child of prophecy

Biography

Born with the Nine-Tailed Fox sealed within him, Naruto Uzumaki rose from a lonely outcast to become the 7th Hokage of the Hidden Leaf. He embodies the "Will of Fire," having unified the Five Great Nations and ended the cycle of hatred through sheer determination. A master of the Rasengan and Shadow Clone Jutsu, his journey is a testament to the power of human connection and the resolve to never give up on one's dreams or friends.
